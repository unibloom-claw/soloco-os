# Soloco OS

> **One Person Company Operating System** — Deploy and govern an AI-native company, alone.
>
> **一人公司操作系统** — 由一个人部署并治理一家 AI 原生公司。

---

## What is Soloco OS? / 什么是 Soloco OS？

Soloco OS is the infrastructure layer that lets a single person **deploy, run, and scale an AI organization**.

It introduces a new category: the **One Person Company (OPC)** — not "one person using many AI tools," but one person owning a complete, governable, expandable AI organization system.

Soloco OS 是让一个人**部署、运转并扩张 AI 组织**的基础设施层。

它定义了一个全新类别：**一人公司（OPC, One Person Company）** —— 不是"一个人用了很多 AI 工具"，而是一个人拥有一套完整的、可治理的、可扩张的 AI 组织系统。

---

## The Problem / 问题

What truly limits solo founders and small teams is not speed on individual tasks — it's the inability to affordably own a full **organizational capability stack**:

- Role division across functions (research, sales, engineering, ops)
- Persistent context and cross-task memory
- Decision delegation and risk escalation
- Audit trails and accountability boundaries

真正限制个人创业者和小团队的，不是某一个任务做得不够快，而是无法低成本拥有一套完整的**组织能力**：

- 跨职能的角色分工（研究、销售、工程、运营）
- 持久化的上下文与跨任务记忆
- 决策委托与风险上报机制
- 行为记录与责任边界

> Hiring, outsourcing, and buying SaaS are all ways of purchasing organizational structure. That structure remains expensive, slow, and deeply human-dependent.
>
> 雇人、外包、购买 SaaS，本质上都是在购买组织结构。这套组织结构至今仍然昂贵、缓慢，且高度依赖人力。

## The Vision / 愿景

When AI can carry execution responsibility, the human role shifts from **managing tasks** to **governing a system** — setting goals, defining constraints, approving critical decisions, handling exceptions.

当 AI 能够承担执行工作，人类的角色从**管理任务**转向**治理系统** —— 设定目标、定义边界、批准关键决策、处理异常情况。

> *You don't need to hire a company to deploy one.*
>
> *你不需要雇佣一家公司，也可以部署一家公司。*

---

## Core Concepts / 核心概念

### AI Workers

After you input a goal, Soloco OS spins up a set of AI workers with distinct responsibilities — research, sales, content, engineering, operations, finance, risk. These are not one-off chat threads. They are **persistent organizational entities** with:

- Assigned roles and scoped responsibilities
- Structured memory and task queues
- Collaborative workflows and handoff protocols
- Full audit logs

输入目标后，Soloco OS 启动一组具备不同职责的 AI workers：研究、销售、内容、工程、运营、财务、风控。它们不是一次性的聊天线程，而是**持续存在的组织实体**，具备：

- 明确的角色定义与职责边界
- 结构化记忆与任务队列
- 协作工作流与任务交接协议
- 完整的行为审计日志

### Human Governance Layer / 人类治理层

You don't need to stay online. The AI organization executes in the background. When the system encounters risk, resource allocation decisions, or strategic forks — it routes control back to you.

你不需要一直在线。AI 组织在后台持续执行；当遇到风险、资源分配或战略选择时，系统将决策权交还给你。

### Why AI Organizations Are Different / AI 组织的本质不同

| Dimension / 维度 | Traditional Org / 传统组织 | AI Org / AI 组织 |
|---|---|---|
| Context sync / 上下文同步 | Expensive / 昂贵（会议、文档） | Structured, near-zero friction / 结构化，摩擦趋零 |
| Trust mechanism / 信任机制 | Personal relationships / 个人信任 | Observable, auditable behavior / 可观测、可审计 |
| Management model / 管理模式 | Manage individuals / 管理下属 | Govern a system / 治理系统 |
| Scale ceiling / 规模上限 | Headcount-bound / 受人数限制 | Protocol-bound / 受协议约束 |

## Long-Term Vision / 长期愿景

### Composable AI Organization Network / 可组合的 AI 组织网络

OPC is the first step. When AI organizations can collaborate, subcontract, nest, and compose — a new economic network emerges.

OPC 只是第一步。当 AI 组织可以互相协作、分包、嵌套和组合时，一种全新的经济网络将出现。

Each node is deployable by one person. Collaboration between nodes doesn't require employment contracts or negotiations — it runs on **protocols, permissions, audit, and settlement mechanisms**.

每个节点都可以由一个人部署。节点之间的协作不依赖雇佣合同或谈判，而运行在**协议、权限、审计与结算机制**之上。

Not a traditional company. Not a platform. Not a DAO. A **composable AI organization network**.

这不是传统公司，不是平台，也不是 DAO。这是**可组合的 AI 组织网络**。

---

## Why Soloco OS / 为什么是 Soloco OS

Soloco OS is not an AI organization inside this network — it is the infrastructure that lets AI organizations be **created, operated, governed, and expanded**.

Soloco OS 不是这个网络里的一个 AI 组织，而是让 AI 组织得以**被创建、运行、治理和扩张**的基础设施。

Like Windows is not an app, and AWS is not an internet company, Soloco OS is the layer everyone builds on.

就像 Windows 不是一个应用，AWS 不是一家互联网公司，Soloco OS 是所有人都在上面构建的那一层底座。

**Long-term goal: become the operating system for AI organizations.**

**长期目标：成为 AI 组织的操作系统。**
