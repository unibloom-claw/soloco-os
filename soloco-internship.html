What We're Building

From personal productivity tools
to organizational Agent OS.

In 2025, AI crossed a threshold. Models can now execute tasks continuously, use tools, and coordinate to deliver results — not just answer questions. The real opportunity isn't a better assistant. It's a fundamentally different way to run an organization.

Soloco is the operating system for that shift. Input a goal, and Soloco deploys a coordinated team of AI workers — research, sales, engineering, operations — each with defined roles, persistent memory, task queues, and audit trails. You don't manage tasks. You govern a system.

Whether you're a solo founder building a One-Person Company, or a small business replacing expensive overhead with intelligent infrastructure — Soloco gives you organizational capability that used to require an entire team.

The Team

Built at the frontier.

Soloco's founding team comes from NTU, Imperial College London, Oxford, Columbia, and HKU — with deep backgrounds in AI agent systems, LLM applications, and autonomous systems including mobile robotics and autonomous driving. We're operating where the research ends and the real product begins. Seed-funded, moving fast, and building the infrastructure layer that defines how organizations work in the age of AI.
